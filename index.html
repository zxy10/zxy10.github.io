About

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Software Engineering at Taiyuan University of Technology, and currently a member of the Lyency Lab. I have participated in projects including large language model development integrated with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) and FastAPI backend development . Additionally, I actively explore the field of deep learning and have completed a research project that enhances general semantic segmentation by combining SAM with DINOv3 .
